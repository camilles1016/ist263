Recently Released

The Last Goodbye by ODESZA and Bettye LaVette

This song was ODESZA's first release after a four year silence since their last album A Moment Apart. This song is a perfect house/EDM tune that has a really good beat drop and is perfect to dance to.

death wish by ROLE MODEL

ROLE MODEL's song was released in September of 2021. This song was released as a single and is different to most of his other tracks. It is a bit more depressing and “real”.

Blow The Roof by Louis The Child, Kasbo, and EVAN GIIA

Louis The Child is not only one of my favorite artists but EVAN GIIA is also a fan favorite. Her voice can match perfectly to Louis The Child and Kasbo's great beats. This song is a perfect, happy song and features great artists.

Invincible by Omar Apollo and Daniel Caesar

Daniel Caesar being one of my favorite R&B artists releases a new song with Omar Apollo. Both these artist are a staple and never go wrong.

I Hate U by SZA

This song started on TikTok before it was released. A line in the chorus became overplayed before I had even heard the full song. When the song was released I fell back in love. SZA is such a favorite and never fails.

AMAZING by Rex Orange County

Rex Orange County has a soft spot in my heart. All their songs give me such comfort and I feel as though I could listen to him at any time of day. This song Is their first single since their last release in 2017.

Don't Forget My Love by Diplo and Miguel

This is Diplo's first solo album release in 18 years. I think the match of Diplo and Miguel is a perfect mix of house and R&B.
